# Smoove - Product Information

## Product Overview

**Product Name:** Smoove

**Tagline:** "Let's Smoove Forward" - Making life easier, business more profitable

**Category:** All-in-One Marketing Automation & Email Management Platform

**Website:** https://www.smoove.io

---

## Positioning

Smoove is an all-in-one digital marketing system designed for small businesses, freelancers, and enterprises to streamline operations, automate customer engagement, and increase profitability through intelligent marketing automation and customer relationship management.

**Core Value Proposition:**
A complete marketing ecosystem that replaces multiple tools with one simple, efficient platform. Enables businesses to create personalized customer journeys based on data, automate workflows, and focus on growth instead of manual tasks.

---

## Key Features

### Core Capabilities
- **Email Marketing** - Customizable templates, automation sequences, and campaign management
- **Marketing Automation** - Drag-and-drop workflow builder for customer journeys
- **Landing Page Builder** - No-code page creation for lead generation
- **SMS & WhatsApp Business Messaging** - Multi-channel customer communication
- **Contact Management (CRM)** - Centralized customer database and relationship tracking
- **Digital Course Management** - Platform for creating and selling online courses
- **Analytics & Tracking** - Campaign performance metrics and customer behavior insights
- **A/B Testing** - Optimize campaigns with split testing (Standard plan+)
- **API Access** - Custom integrations and third-party connections

### Advanced Features (Higher Tiers)
- Image opening tracking
- Automation integrations with external tools
- Multiple user accounts (team collaboration)
- Custom domain support
- Dedicated customer support

## Pricing Structure

All pricing in Israeli Shekel (₪). Annual billing available for discount.

| Plan | Monthly Cost | Annual Cost | Best For |
|------|-------------|------------|----------|
| **Free** | ₪0 | N/A | Getting started, 100 free contacts |
| **Basic** | ₪69 | ₪63/mo | Small businesses, essential tools |
| **Standard** | ₪74 | ₪67/mo | Growing businesses, e-commerce |
| **Pro** | ₪148 | ₪134/mo | Enterprises, advanced features |

### Plan Features Breakdown

**Free Plan**
- Landing pages, email marketing, automation, CRM, surveys
- 100 free contacts included
- No credit card required
- SMS not included

**Basic Plan (+)**
- 3 SMS sends/month (unlimited characters)
- WhatsApp (additional charge)
- Smart links, tracking codes
- Up to 100-20K+ contacts (scalable)

**Standard Plan (+++)**
- 5 SMS sends/month
- E-commerce tools
- 5 team users
- Custom domain support
- A/B testing capabilities

**Pro Plan (++++++)**
- 7 SMS sends/month
- Image opening tracking
- Dedicated customer support
- 10 team users
- Custom footer options

### Contact Scaling
All plans scale based on contact volume:
- 100 contacts (Free/entry level)
- Up to 20,000+ contacts (on higher plans)

## Target Customers

### Primary Audience Segments

#### 1. **Freelancers & Solo Entrepreneurs**
- **Pain Point:** Juggling multiple tasks while losing customers to poor follow-up
- **Needs:** Simple automation, affordable pricing, email + SMS capabilities
- **Benefit:** Automated customer journey management without hiring staff

#### 2. **E-commerce Managers & Online Store Owners**
- **Pain Point:** Low website conversion, cart abandonment, declining ROI
- **Needs:** Email automation, SMS recovery campaigns, landing pages, A/B testing
- **Benefit:** Increase sales through targeted automation, cart recovery, customer retention

#### 3. **Marketing Managers & Growth Teams**
- **Pain Point:** Generating quality leads and maintaining customer loyalty
- **Needs:** Workflow automation, CRM integration, multi-channel messaging, analytics
- **Benefit:** Data-driven customer engagement, measurable results, team collaboration

#### 4. **Small to Medium Businesses (SMBs)**
- **Pain Point:** Multiple tool subscriptions, complex integrations, steep learning curve
- **Needs:** All-in-one solution, ease of use, cost efficiency
- **Benefit:** Consolidate tools, reduce subscription costs, faster implementation

#### 5. **Digital Course Creators & Online Educators**
- **Pain Point:** Managing students, email communication, automation
- **Needs:** Course platform, email marketing, student management
- **Benefit:** Complete platform for course delivery and student engagement
